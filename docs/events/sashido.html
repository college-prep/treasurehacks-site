How to Get Started

To get started with SashiDo platform, just follow these 5 easy steps!

  1. Register for SashiDo with just a few clicks.
  2. Enter your promo code in the Coupons section and apply it.
  3. Enter a valid credit card and billing contact info in the Payment Method section. (You won’t get charged from that card until your credits are exhausted).
  4. Follow the Getting Started Guide to get familiar with our platform in no time!
  5. Join our Slack channel where you can meet other SashiDo interns as well as hackathon and ML challenge participants to help each other, discuss, and collaborate.

In case you have any questions or you need help setting up your account, SashiDo’s team will be more than happy to assist you, just send an email at support@sashido.io.

Machine Learning Challenge

Are you planning a building an application using Machine Learning? Our sponsor SashiDo is offering a special prize for the most original Image project build with SashiDo & Teachable Machine by Google.

How to enter SashiDo’s challenge:

  1. Register for SashiDo 45-day Free trial
  2. Build your project with Teachable Machine & SashiDo.
  3. Here is a useful Teachable Machine Node that empowers you to load any image classification model trained with Google's Teachable Machine tool in a Node.JS project.
  4. Publish a short post on https://medium.com/ about your project (include a description of the idea and how you have implemented it, link to repo/dev post, team members, a short description of the technologies you used, and any other details you might find interesting)
  5. Submit the link to your https://medium.com/ post to hello@sashido.io by the end of the 30th of Oct, 2021 to enter the competition, get your project in front of the entire SashiDo's audience and win amazing prizes.
  6. Don't forget to sign up for early access to TeachableHub where you will be able to upload, share, and download ML models as APIs for FREE!
